CleverCash wallets look like most of the wallets you've known and loved until now - they're made of real leather, and hold your money and cards just the same. But our wallets are also different in a fundamental way.

We at CleverCash know that losing your wallet can be more than an inconvenience. That's why our wallets are designed with a focus on being easily retrievable in case you do experience this all too common issue. CleverCash wallets are designed with built-in, lightweight GPS technology that works with your smartphone to help you locate your wallet the moment it's known to be missing.
